Welcome to Solitaire!

How To Play

  • Click on the card you wish to move, then click where you want to move it.
  • If the card is highlighted, it is selected.
  • If the card does not move, the move was invalid.
  • You can also double-click a card to move it to the top foundation stacks.

Game Rules

  • The goal is to stack all cards in the top foundation stacks by suit from Ace to King.
  • Cards in the tableau must be stacked in alternating colour order in descending rank.
  • Only a king can be placed in an empty tableau space.
